About Me

My 7 years experience consists mainly of SOAP Services implementations and integration solutions with Mule ESB for an automotive industry and (currently) providing RESTful backend for front-end applications. I care about software craftsmanship and I always makes high quality, readable code my priority.

Apart from coding I love to spend my time on traveling and taking photographs. I’m a co-author of a photo blog - ontherun.pl.
